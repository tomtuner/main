The Campus Life Reservations Office handles all RIT student, faculty and staff reservations for Campus Center (building 3) and Student Alumni Union (building 4) meeting rooms, display cases and tables. Campus Life Reservations also processes all non-credit bearing student requests for academic space through the Registrars Office if space is not available in the SAU or Campus Center. Nonaffiliated requests must be made through RIT’s Office of Government and Community Relations by calling 585.475.5012.

If you are interested in reserving a room in the Campus Center or SAU, please contact us by phone at 585.475.6992 or email us at reserve@rit.edu. A new online form for room reservations is currently undergoing construction.

Visit the Room Descriptions page to learn more about each of the rooms in the Campus Center and SAU.
